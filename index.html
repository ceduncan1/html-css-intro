Glassblowing

Glassblowing is awesome. It all began in Italy centuries ago, but didn't become an artistic medium of much importance in the United States until the middle of the twentieth century.

Here are some of my favorite current glass artists:

  • Dale Chihuly
  • Marvin Lipofsky
  • Stephen Rolfe Powell
  • Lino Tagliapietra

Here they are in order of my favorite to my only slightly less favorite:

  1. Stephen Rolfe Powell
  2. Lino Tagliapietra
  3. Dale Chihuly
  4. Marvin Lipofsky
